SPORTS

  • Table Tennis: I love the speed and focus required in table tennis. It sharpens my reflexes and helps me unwind while still staying mentally sharp.

  • Chess: Chess challenges my mind and pushes me to think several steps ahead. I enjoy practicing different openings and solving puzzles to improve my strategic thinking.

MUSIC
